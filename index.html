About Me

My name is Amjad (the "J" is NOT silent), it's Arabic and it means Greatest Glory. Born in Wilmington DE, Grew up in the middle east. came back to "DelaWHERE??" after finishing High School. My coding journey started at a very young age in Syria, my first program I "typed" was in QuickBASIC language. I didn’t say "coded" because I didn’t know WTF I was typing! I was 3rd maybe 4th grade my father opened the compiler on our old (still alive) IBM XT 286, showed me a page with letters, numbers and symbols (10 years later I learned it's called "source code") and said: "If you type these lines exactly as they appear, you'll make a PC game". I was curious I compiled it and it ran!! since that time I’ve been fascinated and interested in twisting and manipulating compurters.
